Who We Are

Iksha Designs & Engg. is an ISO certified company for New Product Development, manufacturing of Aluminium, Zinc die casting, Plastic Injection Moulding, and mould manufacturing. We offer our customers End to End solutions ranging from Prototyping, Reverse Engineering, Tool Designing & Manufacturing, Die Casting, Shot Blasting, Vibro Polishing, Powder Coating, Assembly & multi process Foundry services.

Iksha Designs & Engg. delivers best-in-class New Product Development, Aluminium & Zinc Pressure Die Casting, Gravity Die Casting, Plastic Injection Moulding and Mould Design & Development.
