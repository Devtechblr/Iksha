Products & Services

We specialize in New Product Development and precision manufacturing spanning Aluminium & Zinc HPDC, Gravity Die Casting, Plastic Injection Moulding, and complete Mould Design/Development/Maintenance.

Mould Design / Development / Maintenance

Advanced modeling reduces lead time and errors. Engineering assistance for NPD, product design, reverse engineering & manufacturing feasibility. Tools deliver maximum productivity and extended life.

High precision Aluminium & Zinc components with consistent dimensional accuracy and surface finish for demanding applications.

Gravity Die Casting

Reliable, cost-effective casting suited for medium-volume production with excellent mechanical properties.

Plastic Injection Moulding

High-quality parts with tight tolerances, consistent color, and repeatability for automotive, electronics, and consumer applications.

New Product Development

Concept-to-production including prototyping, validation, and scalable manufacturing with rigorous quality processes.
